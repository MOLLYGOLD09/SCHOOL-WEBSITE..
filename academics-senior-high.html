Senior High School

Grades 11 to 12 Specialized Education Tracks

Program Overview

At Silangan National High School, the Senior High School program offers the following strands: Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Home Economics (HE) Industrial Arts (IA) Information and Communication Technology (ICT) These strands are designed to equip students with the necessary knowledge, skills, and competencies to prepare them for higher education, employment, entrepreneurship, and lifelong learning.

Track Overview

Understanding the three main tracks in Senior High School

Academic Track ▼

The Academic Track prepares students for college education through specialized strands that focus on specific fields of study. Students can choose from ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management), HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences), or STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics)

Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track ▼

The TVL Track provides students with technical skills and competencies needed for immediate employment or entrepreneurship. The Information and Communication Technology (ICT) strand equips students with practical skills in computer systems, programming, and digital technologies.

Program Benefits

Advantages of completing Senior High School

🎓 Multiple Paths to Success

Provides students with various options for higher education or immediate employment after graduation.

🔧 Specialized Skills

Develops specific competencies and expertise in chosen fields through focused track and strand programs.

🚀 Career Readiness

Better prepares students for the challenges and demands of college education or the workforce.

💼 Industry-Relevant

Curriculum is designed to meet industry standards and current employment market needs.
